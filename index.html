What's eSport?

ESports is the games sports or being more formal, the Electronic sports, throughout the years this type of sport is becoming the new Soccer, basketball and other most known sports the of the youngs. In 2019, says the Statista, League Of Legends, was the most-watched eSports video game, reaching up to almost 350 million hours watched. Since the pandemics start, by 2020, the gaming community grew up fast, according to Games Magazine Brasil, considering the main eSports platforms, game watching reached almost 30 billion hours watched, 78% more than before the Pandemic outbreak.
