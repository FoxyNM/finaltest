"The Esports industry is fast on its way to becoming the most financially lucrative market on the planet."

Informations taken from

Newzoo, one of the world's most trusted sources for games and esports analytics, says that gaming is the fastest-growing form of entertainment globally, with their revenues at 9%+ per year. The expectations for 2022 are that the global video game market surpass $190 billion in revenues. Esports revenues were $856 million in 2018 and are expected to grow to $1.79 billion by 2022.
